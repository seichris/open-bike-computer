# Bike Computer 2.0 Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 9, 2026

Bike Computer 2.0 is a cycling navigation companion app. This policy explains what information the app uses and how it is handled.

## Data We Do Not Collect

Bike Computer 2.0 does not require an account. We do not sell personal data, use advertising SDKs, or use third-party analytics SDKs.

## Location

The app uses your device location to show your position, calculate cycling routes, provide turn-by-turn navigation, and track route progress while navigation is active.

Most location processing happens on your device or through Apple system services such as MapKit. We are not responsible for data collected by Apple through Apple frameworks and services.

If you request an offline map pack, the selected map area or route corridor may be sent to the Bike Computer map service so it can build the requested offline map file. This data is used only to provide the requested map functionality and is not used for advertising or tracking.

## Bluetooth and Local Network

The app uses Bluetooth and local network access to connect to compatible bike computer hardware and transfer navigation or map data. This data is used to operate the connected device.

## Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with advertisers or data brokers. Data may be processed by service providers only as needed to operate app functionality, such as generating requested offline map packs.
